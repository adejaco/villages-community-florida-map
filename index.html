About the Project

A map The Villages® can be proud of

The Villages® Community Florida Map is a professionally printed, full-color foldable map of The Villages® — tucked into a 4″ × 6″ booklet that unfolds to a full 32″ × 18″ community reference. It's the kind of thing residents keep in their golf cart, car, give to visiting family, or pick up at a local event.

The map features 33 business card-sized advertising spots. Ad revenue covers the cost of printing. The maps are then distributed by local non-profits and youth sports organizations, who sell them to raise funds for their own causes. Every copy sold puts money back into the community.

How it all works

How many maps will be printed?

The print run is 5,000 to 10,000 copies, depending on total revenue from ad sales and donations. Printing costs are approximately $7,400 for 5,000 maps and $11,000 for 10,000 maps. More revenue means more maps — and more fundraising potential for every partner.

Is there any upfront cost for non-profit partners?

No. Ad sales from all participating organizations collectively cover printing. Your organization takes on zero financial risk.

How does map inventory get divided among partners?

Map allocation is proportional to what each organization raised in ad sales. If your organization accounts for 13.5% of total ad revenue, you receive 13.5% of the maps printed.

What does the ad artwork process look like?

Advertisers submit standard business card artwork (3.5″ × 2″). We handle placement and printing. If you don't have existing artwork, your business card design works perfectly.

What's the difference between the 2024 map and the 2026 map?

The 2024 map was produced and sold exclusively by Habitat for Humanity of Lake-Sumter. The 2026 map adds 33 business advertising spots and opens the distribution model to multiple non-profit and youth sports partners — so more organizations benefit, and a larger print run is possible.
